Affiliate Strategy

A KPI-anchored affiliate programme where every deal structure is derived from live casino performance data. CPA ceilings are calculated from player LTV, revenue share tiers are earned through traffic quality, and all deals are subject to quarterly automated review against current KPIs.

📅 May 2026
👤 Owner: Marketing Manager
📋 Version: 1.0
🔄 Review: Quarterly
💡
Core Philosophy

Most affiliate programmes offer flat deals negotiated on gut feel. This programme does the opposite — every deal parameter is anchored to a live casino KPI. CPA offers are capped at a percentage of actual player LTV. Revenue share tiers are earned, not given. Deals that generate low-quality traffic are automatically downgraded. Deals that consistently outperform unlock better terms. The programme pays for value delivered, not volume promised.

LTV-Anchored CPA Calculator

Every CPA offer starts with the player LTV calculation. The CPA ceiling is the maximum we can pay per player and still achieve the target contribution margin. Update the inputs below as KPIs change — CPA ceilings must be recalculated at each quarterly review.

CPA Ceiling Formula
Player LTV = Avg Monthly NGR per Player × Avg Active Months
Max CPA = Player LTV × (1 − Target Margin %)
Rev Share Ceiling = Brand NGR Margin % − 20% // minimum 20% brand contribution always protected
Effective CPA ROI = (Player LTV − CPA Paid) / CPA Paid × 100

⚠️
When to Recalculate

CPA ceilings must be recalculated whenever: (1) player LTV shifts by more than ±10% in any monthly report, (2) brand NGR margin drops below 55%, (3) average active months changes by ±0.5, or (4) at every quarterly affiliate deal review. No CPA deal may be agreed above the current calculated ceiling without COO sign-off.

03
Affiliate Tiering System

Affiliates are assigned to one of four tiers based on a rolling 3-month performance assessment. Tier determines deal eligibility, payment terms, account manager access and reporting frequency. Tiers are reviewed quarterly and movement is automatic — affiliates can go up or down.

🔄
Tier Movement Rules

An affiliate must maintain all four criteria for their current tier for two consecutive quarters to hold that tier. Missing any single criterion triggers a one-quarter probation period. Missing the criterion for a second consecutive quarter results in automatic downgrade. Upgrades are applied from the following billing month after a successful quarterly review. Tier 4 status requires Marketing Manager and COO approval.

04
Traffic Quality Score (TQS)

The Traffic Quality Score (TQS) is a 0–100 composite score calculated monthly for every affiliate. It determines tier eligibility and deal ceiling. Affiliates with low TQS receive reduced deal terms regardless of volume. High TQS unlocks better rates and faster payment.

TQS Composite Formula
TQS = (Retention Score × 0.30) + (Deposit Quality Score × 0.25) + (Bonus Behaviour Score × 0.20) + (NGR Contribution Score × 0.15) + (Fraud Score × 0.10)
Weight: 30%

Clawback Provisions & Deal Penalties

CPA commissions are not unconditional. Players must meet minimum quality thresholds within the qualification window or the commission is partially or fully clawed back. This protects the brand from paying for dormant, abusive or fraudulent sign-ups.

🔴
Non-Negotiable Clawback Triggers

The following trigger full CPA clawback regardless of tier or deal history: fraudulent account confirmed; player self-excluded within 14 days of FTD; player account linked to existing account or banned device; chargeback raised within 30 days of FTD.
