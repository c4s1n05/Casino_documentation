SOP — VIP Player Identification

Defines how the VIP team identifies newly registered players who exhibit high-value deposit patterns and qualifies them for VIP status. Includes the VIP Identification Matrix, scoring system, four-tier structure, outreach process and early monitoring standards.

📅 May 2026
👤 Owner: VIP Manager
🔄 Review: Quarterly
⏱ Response SLA: 2 hours from trigger
♛
Core Principle

A high-value player identified within their first 72 hours is worth many times more than one contacted at day 30. Speed of identification and quality of first contact are the two most commercially significant variables in a VIP player's lifetime value. This SOP is designed to ensure no high-value player goes unnoticed — and that when they are spotted, the response is fast, personalised and professional.

Matrix Usage Rules

Always use the highest response level from any signal combination — never average them down. If a player triggers ⚡ INSTANT in any cell, that is the response regardless of other cells. The matrix is a minimum standard — VIP team members may escalate any player at their discretion if their instinct or experience suggests higher value potential. Document any discretionary escalations in the player notes.

Initial Outreach — First Contact Standards

The first contact with a newly identified VIP is the most important interaction in the relationship. It sets the tone for everything that follows. It must feel personal, professional and valuable — never generic, pushy or automated.

🚫
What Not to Do

Never send an automated email as the first VIP contact. Never mention that you are contacting them because of a specific deposit amount — this can feel intrusive. Never make promises about benefits before the tier is confirmed and approved.
