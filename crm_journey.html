Communication Types — Reference

📰 News Emails

Specific to the player's level with an upsell side message promoting the next tier.

🎯 Promotion Emails

Generally generic to all levels depending on the promotion. E.g. a GPPT promotional email will list all the different level sats to appeal to all player tiers.

💚 Low Value Offers

Specific to level of lapsing player. Recycle ~4 offers. By "low value" refers to the RB%.

e.g. Earn 100 points before X and get a $5 ticket

🟡 Mid Value Offers

Specific to level of lapsing player.

e.g. Earn 50 points before X and get a $5 ticket

🌙 Moon on a Stick Offer

High value offer, easy to obtain. Used for inactive players to maximise reactivation.

e.g. Earn 25 points before X and get a $5 ticket

📊 DWAG Tiers

DWAG1–5 represent player value bands. Higher DWAG = higher value player. MTT frequency is 48 hrs; SNG/Cash is 72 hrs minimum across all active segments.
